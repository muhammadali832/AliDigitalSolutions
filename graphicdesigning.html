Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats will I receive for my designs?

We provide all final designs in multiple formats including high-resolution PDF, PNG, JPG, and vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) when applicable. For print materials, we also provide print-ready PDFs with proper bleeds and crop marks. We'll discuss your specific needs to ensure you receive all necessary file types.

How many design concepts do you provide?

For logo and branding projects, we typically present 3-5 initial concepts. For other design projects, we present 2-3 concepts depending on the scope. These concepts represent distinct creative directions for you to choose from. We then refine the selected concept based on your feedback.

Do you provide printing services?

While we focus on design, we have established relationships with premium printers and can coordinate printing on your behalf. We'll ensure your designs are print-ready and can recommend the best printing options based on your budget, quantity, and quality requirements.

What if I need revisions to the designs?

We include a set number of revision rounds in our proposals (typically 2-3 rounds). After presenting initial concepts, we incorporate your feedback and refine the selected design. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds can be accommodated at an additional cost if needed.

How do you ensure my design will stand out?

Our process includes thorough market and competitor research to identify opportunities for differentiation. We combine this insight with creative expertise to develop designs that are both distinctive and appropriate for your industry. Our focus is on creating designs that effectively communicate your unique value proposition.

Can you design for both print and digital?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating designs that work seamlessly across both print and digital mediums. We understand the technical requirements for each medium and ensure your branding remains consistent while optimizing for each specific application.
