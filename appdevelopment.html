Frequently Asked Questions

Should I build a native or cross-platform app?

Native apps (separate iOS and Android) offer the best performance and access to all device features but cost more. Cross-platform (React Native/Flutter) provides nearly native performance with significant cost savings. We'll recommend the best approach based on your budget, timeline, and feature requirements.

How much does it cost to develop a mobile app?

App development costs vary widely based on complexity, features, and platforms. Simple apps start around $15,000, medium complexity apps range $30,000-$70,000, and complex enterprise apps can be $100,000+. We provide detailed estimates after understanding your specific requirements.

How long does app development take?

Development timelines depend on app complexity. A basic app takes 2-3 months, medium complexity 3-6 months, and sophisticated apps 6+ months. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation and planning phase.

Will my app work on all devices?

Yes, we ensure your app works across all relevant device sizes and OS versions. For iOS, we typically support the current version plus two previous versions. For Android, we support the current version plus three previous versions, optimized for the most popular device sizes.

How do you ensure app security?

We implement multiple security measures including data encryption, secure authentication, code obfuscation, regular security updates, penetration testing, and compliance with platform-specific security guidelines. For financial or healthcare apps, we implement additional specialized security protocols.

Can you help with app marketing after launch?

Yes, we offer comprehensive app marketing services including App Store Optimization (ASO), social media marketing, influencer partnerships, paid advertising campaigns, and analytics to track user acquisition and engagement. We can also integrate analytics tools to measure performance.
