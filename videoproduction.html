Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a professional video production cost?

Video production costs vary based on complexity, length, crew size, equipment needs, and post-production requirements. Simple interview videos might start around $2,000, while high-end commercials with multiple locations can range from $10,000 to $50,000+. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific project requirements.

How long does it take to produce a video?

The timeline depends on the project scope. A simple 1-2 minute corporate video might take 2-3 weeks from concept to delivery, while a complex commercial with multiple locations could take 6-8 weeks. Animation projects often require additional time (4-8 weeks) depending on the complexity of the artwork and motion.

Do you provide scriptwriting services?

Yes, our team includes experienced scriptwriters who can craft compelling narratives tailored to your brand voice and objectives. We develop scripts that engage your target audience while effectively communicating your key messages. Scriptwriting is included in our full-service video production packages.

Can you help with video distribution and marketing?

Absolutely! Beyond production, we offer strategic guidance on video distribution across platforms (website, social media, YouTube, etc.). We can also create optimized versions for different platforms and provide recommendations for paid promotion to maximize your video's reach and impact.

What if I need changes after seeing the first edit?

We include a set number of revision rounds in our proposals (typically 2 rounds). After delivering the first edit, we incorporate your feedback and make adjustments. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds can be accommodated at an additional cost if needed.

Do you offer live streaming services?

Yes, we provide professional live streaming services for corporate events, product launches, webinars, and more. Our packages include multi-camera setups, professional audio, graphics integration, and platform management to ensure high-quality live broadcasts.
