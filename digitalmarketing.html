Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

The timeline for seeing results varies depending on the strategies implemented. PPC campaigns can generate immediate traffic, while SEO typically takes 3-6 months to show significant results. Social media marketing often begins showing engagement within weeks, but building a substantial following takes longer. We provide realistic timelines during our strategy development phase based on your specific goals and industry.

What makes your approach different from other agencies?

We combine data-driven strategies with creative execution, focusing on delivering measurable ROI rather than vanity metrics. Our team stays ahead of industry changes through continuous training and certification. We also provide complete transparency with detailed reporting and regular strategy reviews. Unlike many agencies, we don't use cookie-cutter solutions—every strategy is customized to your unique business needs.

Do you work with businesses in my industry?

We have experience across numerous industries including e-commerce, professional services, healthcare, technology, and more. Our strategies are tailored to each industry's unique challenges and opportunities. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your specific industry landscape and how we can help you stand out from competitors.

How do you measure the success of campaigns?

We establish clear KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) at the beginning of each campaign based on your business objectives. These typically include metrics like website traffic, lead generation, conversion rates, customer acquisition cost, and ROI. You'll receive monthly reports with these metrics along with our analysis and recommendations for optimization.

Can I cancel my contract if I'm not satisfied?

We're confident in our ability to deliver results, which is why we offer flexible contract terms. Most of our clients start with a 3-month minimum commitment to allow sufficient time for strategies to take effect. After this period, you can cancel with 30 days' notice. However, we'll work closely with you throughout to ensure you're completely satisfied with our services.
